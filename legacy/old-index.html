Costing formula
Factory Cost   = Raw Material × 1.65
Selling Price = Factory ÷ (1 − Margin %)
Total Value    = Selling Price × Quantity
Variance %    = (AI − Manual) ÷ Manual × 100
Amber flag    = |Variance| > 20%
Red flag      = |Variance| > 30%
Confidence <45% = Claude AI offered
